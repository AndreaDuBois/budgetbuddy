What is Pocket Money?

Pocket Money is a family budgeting tool built around shopping events — like back-to-school shopping, holiday gifts, or any time you're giving kids a spending budget. Parents set the budgets, kids upload their receipts, and the app tracks how much has been spent vs. what was allowed.

For Parents

  • Create a Shopping Event — give it a name (e.g. "Back to School 2025") and add categories like Shoes, Shirts, Pants, or School Supplies.
  • Set Budgets — assign a dollar amount per category for each child. You can give different amounts to different kids.
  • Add Family Members — each child gets their own login. You can also invite a co-parent to share full access.
  • Review Receipts — when a kid uploads a receipt, you can see every line item, confirm which category it belongs to, and approve it before totals are updated.
  • Track Everything — the dashboard shows a live budget bar for every kid across every event, so you always know where things stand.

For Kids

  • See Your Budget — a color-coded progress bar shows how much you've spent and how much is left in each category.
  • Upload Receipts 4 Ways — snap a photo, upload a PDF, paste the receipt text, or type items in manually.
  • AI Does the Reading — when you upload a photo or PDF, the app reads the receipt automatically and fills in the amounts.
  • Instant Feedback — you'll see right away if you're close to going over budget in any category.

How a Typical Flow Looks

Step 1 — Parent creates a Shopping Event and adds categories.

Step 2 — Parent sets a budget per category for each child (e.g. $60 for Shoes).

Step 3 — Kid goes shopping and snaps a photo of each receipt.

Step 4 — AI reads the receipt and suggests categories for each item.

Step 5 — Parent (or kid) confirms the line items, and the budget bar updates automatically.

Accounts & Access

  • The first person to set up the app becomes the primary parent.
  • Additional parents can be invited with a shareable link — they get the same full access.
  • Kids each have their own login and can only see their own budgets and receipts.
  • Parents can optionally allow a child to adjust their own category budgets.
