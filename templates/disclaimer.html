AI Receipt Scanning

Pocket Money uses an AI service (Groq) to automatically read and interpret receipt images and documents. This technology is helpful but not guaranteed to be accurate. The AI may:

  • Misread handwritten or low-quality receipts
  • Miss line items or combine them incorrectly
  • Assign items to the wrong budget category
  • Occasionally produce incorrect prices or totals

Always review the AI's output on the confirmation screen before approving. Nothing updates your budget until you explicitly confirm each receipt.

Not Financial Advice

Pocket Money is a personal budgeting and record-keeping tool. It does not provide financial advice, tax guidance, or investment recommendations. Budget amounts and spending limits are set entirely by the family using the app.

Data Storage & Privacy

All data entered into Pocket Money — including names, email addresses, budget amounts, and receipt images — is stored locally on the server or device where the app is running. Pocket Money does not operate a central cloud database or sell any user data.

Receipt content (images, PDF text, pasted text) is sent to Groq's API for processing. Please review Groq's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle submitted content. Avoid uploading receipts containing sensitive personal information beyond standard purchase details.

No Warranty

Pocket Money is provided as-is, without warranty of any kind. The creators are not liable for any inaccuracies in budget tracking, data loss, or decisions made based on information displayed in the app. Always keep your own records of purchases and receipts.

Account Security

You are responsible for keeping your login credentials secure. Use a strong, unique password. If you share a device with your children, sign out after each session. Pocket Money cannot recover a lost password without a parent manually resetting it.
